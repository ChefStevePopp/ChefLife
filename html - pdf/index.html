Our Attendance Standard

We believe in each other, and that means holding ourselves to a standard that reflects our commitment to excellence. As a team, we maintain 94% attendance or higher - not because we demand it, but because we've chosen to be the kind of professionals who show up for ourselves and each other.

If attendance falls below this standard, it's time for an honest conversation about fit and commitment. These discussions aren't punitive - they're about alignment. We want every team member to thrive here, and consistent presence is the foundation of that success.

We're here to support your growth, but we can't invest in someone who isn't fully invested in themselves.
