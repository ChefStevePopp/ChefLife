# PROMISE: Fast Entry, Not Photo Gymnastics

> *L6 in action: We won't make you fight your phone to save 30 seconds.*

---

## The Problem

Every restaurant app promises "just snap a photo!" for receipt capture. The reality:

- Contort your phone to get a flat, well-lit shot
- Wait for OCR processing
- Review line-by-line because OCR misread "Romaine" as "Romain" and "$24.50" as "$2450"
- Fix the errors manually anyway
- Total time: 3+ minutes of frustration

The promise of automation becomes the reality of verification. You're not *entering* data — you're *auditing a robot's homework*.

---

## Why OCR Fails for Restaurants

| Receipt Type | OCR Accuracy | Reality |
|--------------|--------------|---------|
| Clean PDF invoice | 90-95% | Still fixing 1 in 10 items |
| Printed thermal receipt | 70-85% | Faded ink, crinkled paper |
| Handwritten farmer's market | 30-50% | Basically useless |
| Cash & carry register tape | 60-75% | Abbreviations, missing context |

Even at 90% accuracy, you're verifying AND correcting. That's slower than just entering it right the first time.

---

## The ChefLife Way

**Direct entry is faster than photo → process → verify → correct.**

### Mobile Quick Invoice Flow

1. **Pick vendor** (recent vendors at top)
2. **See their items** (filtered to that vendor's MIL products)
3. **Tap → enter qty + price** (or just qty if price known)
4. **Running total** shows you're on track
5. **Save** — done in 30 seconds

No photo. No waiting. No corrections. Just intentional entry.

### Photo Capture for Audit Trail (Not OCR)

We *do* support photos — but as **proof**, not processing:

- Snap the receipt for your records
- Photo attaches to invoice
- Audit gold forever
- **You still enter the data** — because you're faster than any robot

## The Philosophy

### We Respect Your Time (L6)

L6 means: *don't make users work for the software*. 

OCR makes you:
- Work to get a good photo
- Wait for processing
- Work again to verify/correct

Direct entry lets you:
- Enter it once, enter it right
- Be done in 30 seconds
- Move on with your day

### We Respect Your Expertise

You know what you bought. You know what it cost. You don't need a robot guessing — you need a fast interface that captures what you already know.

### We Don't Chase Gimmicks

OCR sounds impressive in a demo. It fails in a farmer's market parking lot with a crinkled receipt and glare on your screen.

**We build for the Friday rush, not the trade show booth.**

## Future Consideration: OCR as Premium Feature

If OCR matures and accuracy reaches 98%+ for clean typed invoices:

- Position as **premium add-on** for high-volume operators
- **Typed invoices only** — PDFs and clean printed receipts
- **Suggestion mode** — pre-fill for user approval, never auto-commit
- **Never for handwritten/thermal** — don't set false expectations

But the manual flow remains the default. Fast, intentional, reliable.
